Björn Stenger

I am a research scientist at the Rakuten Institute of Technology, where I work on computer vision and machine learning. My current work is on 3D reconstruction, visual search, image super-resolution, and video understanding.

Prior to Rakuten I worked at Toshiba Research Europe (see my previous page). I completed my PhD in Prof. Roberto Cipolla's group at the University of Cambridge and my Diplom at the University of Bonn. I spent time at the University of Victoria, at Hewlett-Packard, and at Siemens Corporate Research.
