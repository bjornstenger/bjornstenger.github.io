Bjorn Stenger obtained his Diplom (Computer Science) from the University of Bonn, Germany, in 2000 and his Ph.D. degree (Engineering) from the University of Cambridge, UK, in 2004. From 2004-2006 he worked as a Research Fellow at the Toshiba R&D Center in Japan. In 2006 he joined the Computer Vision Group of Toshiba Research Europe and became group leader in 2009. He worked on human-computer interfaces, 3D capture, and object recognition. He joined the Rakuten Institute of Technology in 2015, where he is the lead researcher in the visual computing group. His current research interests include 3D reconstruction, visual search, image super-resolution, and video understanding.
